2. Legal basis (UK / EU GDPR)

3. Where your data is stored

PILLAR uses Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, Analytics). Firebase is operated by Google LLC in the United States, with regional data centres. Project ID: pvaapp-164bf. Google's data processing complies with UK/EU GDPR and the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. See Firebase Privacy & Security.

For UK / EEA / Swiss residents we rely on Google's Standard Contractual Clauses for the international transfer.

4. Crash reporting

Crash reports contain only the error, device model, OS, and app version — never your name, email, or personal content. Opt out: Settings → Privacy → Crash reporting.

5. Your rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, or object to processing. Email eanieakwetey.eaa@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

6. Account deletion

Open the app → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete my account. This removes your Firestore document, on-device storage, and Firebase Auth record. Deletion is irreversible.

7. Children

PILLAR is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has registered, email us and we will delete the account.

8. Security

  • All traffic over TLS / HTTPS.
  • Firebase data at rest encrypted with AES-256.
  • Auth tokens on device in iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences.
  • Firestore Security Rules prevent cross-user access.

We will update this page if practices change. Material changes are notified in-app before they take effect.
